Good-or-Mood Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Introduction

Good-or-Mood is a Chrome extension designed to help users track their daily moods and emotional states. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Data Collection

Local Data Storage: All data, including mood scores, events, and calendar records, is stored locally on your browser. This extension does not transmit data to external servers.

What We Collect:

  • Mood scores and related events you log.
  • Daily mood summaries stored locally for calendar visualization.

Data Usage

Your data is solely used to provide the core functionalities of Good-or-Mood. It is not shared, sold, or transferred to any third party.

Permissions

Good-or-Mood requests the following permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your mood logs, events, and extension preferences locally.
  • Host Permissions: These are required for the extension to inject scripts and render the interface on active web pages. No external content is accessed.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. Since all information is stored locally on your browser, ensure that your device is secure to prevent unauthorized access.

Limitations

This extension operates in offline mode and does not access or transmit data to any online services. However, it is your responsibility to ensure that your browser and device are secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. Please review it periodically for updates.
